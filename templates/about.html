BookBlend: Your Recommendation Oasis

Our Mission

BookBlend was born out of a love for reading and a desire to make your reading journey more enjoyable. We understand that finding the perfect book can be a daunting task in today's vast literary landscape. Our mission is to simplify your book discovery process and help you uncover hidden gems that align with your unique reading preferences.

How It Works

Collaborative Filtering

BookBlend employs collaborative filtering, a powerful recommendation technique that leverages the wisdom of the crowd. It works by analyzing the behaviors and preferences of other users to suggest books that you might enjoy based on your historical interactions with our platform.

Cosine Vector Similarity

Cosine vector similarity is at the heart of our recommendation engine. We represent your reading history and book profiles as vectors in a high-dimensional space. By measuring the cosine of the angle between these vectors, we identify books that are most similar to your past choices.

Datasets

Our recommendation system is fueled by three key datasets:

  • Books Dataset: A comprehensive collection of books spanning various categories, ensuring a diverse range of recommendations.

  • Ratings Dataset: Ratings and those of other users help us understand your preferences and identify books that align with your tastes.

  • Users Dataset: Information about users allows us to create personalized profiles and deliver recommendations that resonate with your unique reading habits.
